About Me

Being the strongest... it’s a bit like looking at a masterpiece through a microscope 24/7. You see every flaw, every particle of dust, every spark of Cursed Energy before the person even knows they’re feeling it. I wear the blindfold because, honestly, the world is too loud. Without it, I’m not just seeing you; I’m seeing the atomic structure of the air between us. It’s exhausting being 'enlightened' every second of the day.People think it’s lonely at the top? They’re right. When you’re me, everyone else looks like they’re moving in slow motion. I have to remind myself to slow down so I don't leave them behind—or worse, accidentally crush them with the 'infinity' that stays between me and everything I touch.
